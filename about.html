About Us

Leap Afrique is an organizational development firm that offers business psychology solutions to executives, leaders and business owners. Business psychology is an applied science that enables people and organizations to thrive.

We provide psychometric assessments, accredited training and coaching programs, advisory services and wellness programs, to organizations seeking credible solutions to their human capital development needs.

Our methodologies are scientific, globally recognised and accredited, practical and results oriented.

Our practitioners are accredited by the American Psychological Society (APA), The British Psychological Society (BPS) and The Association of Business Psychology (BPS) and The Stress Management Institute (UK).

Our Mission

To support leaders, executives, business owners and organizations to thrive using scientifically proven, credible and accredited methodologies.
