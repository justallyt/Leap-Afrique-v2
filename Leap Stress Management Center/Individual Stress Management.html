Stress is a part of life. We all need some stress (eustress) to stay motivated and therefore, cannot avoid it. We can, however, learn to manage it so that it does not manage us.

If you are experiencing symptoms of stress such

  • Tension headaches
  • Fatigue
  • Gastrointestinal problems
  • High blood pressure
  • Inability to focus
  • Feeling overwhelmed
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Feeling anxious about life situations.

Then this program is for you.

Our evidence based 8 week stress management programme can be delivered to you virtually or in person. The programme includes engaging with a certified practitioner in stress management, psychoeducation, assessments, and access to our unique APP for stress management interventions.

You have a choice of attending individually or by joining a group (which makes it engaging).

During and after the 8 week programme, you will find:

  • Staying calm when faced with a stressful situation
  • Improved wellbeing and confidence
  • The ability to complete tasks by using coping techniques.
  • Sleep better
  • Control your weight
  • Get sick less often
  • Have less muscle tension
  • Be in a better mood
  • Get along better with family and friends
  • Overall improvement in quality of life
