Leap Stress Management Center

Our bodies are fairly well equipped to handle everyday stressors. However, when not managed well, stress can become chronic with serious consequences both physically and mentally. Physically one may experience tension type headaches, shoulder and neck pain, lower back pain, gastrointestinal discomfort, sleep disturbances and weight loss or gain. Prolonged stress can lead to ailments such as heart attacks/stroke, high blood pressure, ulcers, diabetes and cancer. Studies have shown a link between stress and psychological disorders such as anxiety, depression and substance use.

Our Centre for Stress Management offers you evidence based interventions based on the latest developments in the management of stress. Our practitioners are certified by the Institute of Stress management, UK.
